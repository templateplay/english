In this programme, Catherine and Dan will be discussing whether mermaids are actually real or not, as well as teaching you 6 new items of vocabulary.

This week's question:

When did the most recent alleged mermaid sighting take place? Was it:

a) the 1400s

b) 1800s

or c) 2000s?

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

Vocabulary

said or believed be true but not proven to be true

a different kettle of fish
a completely different matter or issue

hoax
a deception or lie often done for humour or cruelty

serene
calm and peaceful

a getaway
a place you go in order to escape from normal life
