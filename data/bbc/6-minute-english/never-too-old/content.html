Is retirement the end of everything or just a door for new opportunities? Alice and Rob discuss the limits of age and Rob... well, he wants to know how old Alice is.

This week's question

What percentage of people aged over 65 surveyed in the UK described themselves as old? Is it …

a) 6%?

b) 16%?

c) 60%?

You can hear the right answer at the end of the programme.
