Do you secretly watch people when in public? And do you think they're watching you? Neil and Dan explore some fascinating research about 'people-watching' and teach you six items of useful vocabulary in this episode of 6 Minute English. 

This week's question:

OK, question time. Which of these stories features an invisibility cloak?

a) The Hobbit

b) Harry Potter

c) The Invisible Man

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

Vocabulary

invisible
impossible to be seen

cloak
a long piece of material worn over the shoulders like a coat

illusion
something which appears real but isn't

mismatch
difference between two things; a bad combination

judgemental
critical

self-conscious
too aware of yourself; embarrassed what others think about you
