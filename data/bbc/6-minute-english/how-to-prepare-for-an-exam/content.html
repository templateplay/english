Students up and down the UK are busy revising for exams. Alice and Rob consider which study techniques are effective – and which aren't – including Rob's suggestions to sleep with his textbook under his pillow and singing in the shower.

This week's question

What's the word for a system, such as use of special poems or rhymes to help you remember something? Is it …

a) pneumatics

b) mnemonics

c) hypnotics?

You can hear the right answer at the end of the programme.

Vocabulary

revise
study information you learned before

critical
very important

neurons
nerve cells

retrieve
get something back

distributed practice
repeated testing over a longer period of time

cramming
learn lots of information in a short period of time

elaborate
add more information

conceptual
based on ideas
