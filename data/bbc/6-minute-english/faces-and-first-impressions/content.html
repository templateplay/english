Can we trust our first impressions? Scientist Alexander Todorov believes that very often our first impressions are wrong. Neil and Rob take a second look and teach you six items of useful vocabulary in this episode of 6 Minute English.

This week's question:

How long does it takes to form a first impression about someone’s face?

a) under one second

b) one second

c) six seconds

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

Vocabulary

first impression
the idea or opinion of someone or something you make after a very short time

trustworthy
able to be trusted

extroverted
(of a person) happy and enjoying being with others

attributes
qualities

perception
opinion based on how things appear

to go with your gut
make a decision based on feelings
