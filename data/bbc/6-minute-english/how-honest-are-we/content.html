Are you honest? Have you ever cheated anybody? Rob and Neil discuss if honesty comes naturally to people or if it is a product of living in a social group.

This week's question

Who said 'honesty is the best policy'? Was it…

a) Donald Trump

b) Benjamin Franklin

c) Richard Nixon? 

Listen to the programme to find out the answer. 

Vocabulary 

honest
truthful 

cheat
trick or deceive someone to get something you want 

consumer
person who buys things or services 

psychological make up
the way the human mind works, the way we think 

selfish
someone who only cares about themselves and not about other people 

co-operatively
working together towards shared goals
