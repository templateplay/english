Do you have what it takes to be an astronaut? Alice and Rob discuss the challenges of going to space to do a job thousands of people are keen on.

This week's question

How many people have applied to join Nasa's 2017 astronaut class? Was it…

a) 800

b) 8,000

c) 18,000 people?

You can hear the correct answer at the end of the programme.

Vocabulary

in orbit
being in space and following the path around the Earth's curvature

claustrophobia
fear of being in a small space

get on someone's nerves
to annoy someone

zero gravity
a condition where gravity is exerting no force

buoyancy
the ability to float

nosedive
fast and sudden fall

stranded
left behind

forage
to search (used especially for animals looking for food)
