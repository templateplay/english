About 37,000 tourists are expected to visit Antarctica this season. But should they be going to a region with such a sensitive environment? Listen to Rob and Neil's conversation and learn some new vocabulary.

This week's question:

What large resource can be found in Antarctica? That's my question to you today. Is Antarctica:

a)    The world's largest coal field

b)    The world's largest gold source

c)    The world's largest diamond source

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

 

Vocabulary

wilderness
area with no people and no agriculture because the difficult living conditions

to set foot
to go to

ice caps
thick layer of ice covering permanently an area of land

global warming
the increase in world temperatures due to the presence of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere

advocates
people who defend a cause or an idea.

to disinfect
to clean something using chemicals or vacuum to kill or remove bacteria
