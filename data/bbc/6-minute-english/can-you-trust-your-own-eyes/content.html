Can you trust your own memory? Catherine and Neil discuss why the police and the legal system are concerned about eyewitness testimony.

This week's question

In which part of the brain is there an area dedicated to remembering faces? Is it…

a) the temporal lobe?

b) the hypothalamus?

or c) the cerebellum? 

You'll hear the answer at the end of the programme.
