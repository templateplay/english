Are the days of paying by cash for a latte or a newspaper nearly gone? Now you can pay via mobile phone, contactless cards, and even using your finger. Alice and Neil discuss Neil's fondness for loose change while teaching some related vocabulary.

This week's question

Which man's face appears on a UK ten pound note? Is it…

a) Charles Darwin?

b) Isaac Newton?

c) Albert Einstein?

You can hear the right answer at the end of the programme.

Vocabulary

behind the curve
not keeping up with current thinking or trends

tap-and-go
wireless payment technology using credit and debit cards, or mobile phones

antenna
equipment used for sending and receiving radio or TV signals

ether
the air where electronic communication happens

fraud
getting money by cheating people

spending spree
doing a lot of shopping over a short period of time

wearable tech
things you can wear which combine payment and security technology

authenticate
(in general) prove something is true or genuine
